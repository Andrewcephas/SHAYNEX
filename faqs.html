Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common inquiries about Shaynex’s services, operations, and business model.

Which Shaynex service is right for me?

Shaynex offers solutions across multiple industries. Whether you’re looking for design, logistics, supply chain, or general consultancy, our team will work with you to match the service that best fits your needs.

Do I have to commit to a long-term contract?

No, Shaynex offers flexible agreements depending on your service scope. We support both project-based and ongoing partnerships based on your preference.

What payment methods are supported?

We accept a wide range of payment methods including bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and mobile payments. For large-scale partnerships, invoicing and payment schedules can be arranged.

Can I switch services after signing up?

Yes. We understand needs evolve. You can upgrade, scale down, or shift focus across our offerings at any time by speaking with your assigned account manager.

Are there any hidden fees?

No. Shaynex values transparency. All costs are clearly outlined in your proposal or contract. We do not include hidden charges.

How do I get started with Shaynex?

Just reach out through our contact form or request a consultation. Our team will respond quickly to discuss your goals and recommend the right solutions.

Do you offer trials or pilots?

Depending on the service, we may offer a limited-scope pilot to help you evaluate our approach before full engagement. Contact us to learn more.

Can I manage my services online?

Yes, we are currently rolling out a customer dashboard for service management, tracking, and support. You'll receive credentials upon onboarding.

What industries do you serve?

We work across various sectors including logistics, retail, corporate, education, tech, and events. Shaynex adapts to meet the unique needs of different industries.

Need help? Where can I reach support?

You can contact our support team anytime through our website, email, or phone. We’re here to help you every step of the way.

Shaynex - Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services including logistics, training, consultancy, lab services, and general supplies.

What services does Shaynex offer?

Shaynex is a multi-purpose enterprise offering medical laboratory diagnostics, corporate consultancy, logistics and delivery solutions, professional training, and general supplies.

Do I need to schedule an appointment?

For services like lab diagnostics, training, and consultancy, appointments are recommended. However, some services such as supplies and deliveries can be requested on-demand.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept mobile money (M-Pesa), bank transfers, and cash payments. More digital options will be added soon to increase convenience for our clients.

Where is Shaynex located?

We are located at Magsons Plaza, 3rd Floor, Room 13, Kisii. Feel free to walk in during working hours or contact us to book a service.

Are there long-term contracts required?

No, we offer both one-time services and flexible service agreements depending on your business or personal needs.

Can I get customized training or consultancy?

Yes, Shaynex provides tailored training programs and consultancy packages based on the client's industry and goals. Reach out to us for a custom quote.

Do you offer home or office pickups for lab samples?

Yes, our lab team can arrange for sample collection from your home or office. Just schedule your pickup through our website or by calling us directly.

How quickly can I get results from lab tests?

Most routine tests are completed within 24 hours. Urgent tests can be fast-tracked depending on the case. All results are delivered securely and confidentially.

Can I consult a specialist through Shaynex?

Yes. We offer both in-house expertise and access to external specialists across different fields. You can book a consultation session anytime.

How can I get in touch with customer support?

You can reach us via phone, email, WhatsApp, or by visiting our office. Visit the contact page on our website for full details and hours of operation.
